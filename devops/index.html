    DevOps Notes and Resources

    • Germinated as conversations around Agile System Admin practices: @littleidea, @patrickdebois
    • MeetUp organized by Patrick Debois in Ghent, Belgium 2009 Decade Anniversary
    • John Willis aka @Botchagalupe the meta-gist motherlode of DevOps Goodness
    • CALMS: Culture, Automation, Lean, Measurement, Sharing (John Willis/Damon Edwardsi for moree see DevOps Cafe)
    • What is the role of Site Reliability Engineer? SRE Book and SRE Workbook. How SRE relates to DevOps
    • DORA DevOps Data and Metrics singularity Dr. Nicole Forsgren @nicolefv
    • Accelerate (deserves its own entry with DevOps Handbooks)
    • Jez Humble Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery (also DORA and Accelerate)
    • Mark Burgess CFEngine, Promise Theory and how the field of physics informs IT. Burgess pretty much invented modern config management space. By extension heavily influenced Adam Jacob @OPsCode/Chef
    • Config Management Prime-Movers : Luke Kaines (Puppet) and Adam Jacob (Chef) Michael DeHann (Ansible) Mitchell Hashimoto (Terraform) Jeff Snover (PowerShell)
    • Gene Kim (Visible Ops, ITRevolution, DOES) @realgenekim When IT Fails
    • DevOps Enterprise Summits
    • Netflix Culture Deck and Patty McCord TED TALK
    • Adrian Cockroft (Sun/Netflix/AWS) Chaos Engineering and a ton more. Check his talks and dig his book recommendations
    • Podcasts: Cloud Cafe, DevOps Cafe, Arrested Devops, The Ship Show, Software Defined Talk
    • DevOps Readiness Assessment DORA
    • DevOpsDays: Conferences arguably were the structures that germanated the movement's accelerated adoption and cloud. The conferences help ignite the DevOps cultural movement/moment.
    • DevSecOps: James Wickett, Rudgged DevOps Justin Cormack (IAmTheCalvary)
    • Simon Wardley: Wardley Mapping as a strategic tool. It's like he can tell the future and often does.
    • The Emergence of Serverless
