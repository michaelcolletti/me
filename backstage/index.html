Backstage: The Spotify Developer Platform

Q: What is Backstage and why did Spotify create it? A: *Backstage Is an open source framework for building developer portals. Powered by a centralized software catalog, Backstage restores order to your microservices and infrastructure and enables your product teams to ship high-quality code quickly without compromising autonomy. Backstage unifies all your infrastructure tooling, services, and documentation to create a streamlined development environment from end to end. *
